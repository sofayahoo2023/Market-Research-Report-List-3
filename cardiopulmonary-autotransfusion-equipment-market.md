<p><p>Cardiopulmonary Autotransfusion Equipment Market comprises two main types of devices: Unwashed ATS and Washed ATS. </p><p>Unwashed ATS involves collecting blood from a patient during surgery, filtering it to remove contaminants, and then reinfusing it back into the patient without further processing. </p><p>In contrast, Washed ATS involves an additional step of washing the collected blood to remove residual contaminants before reinfusing it back into the patient. Each type has its own advantages and limitations, which contribute to their respective market shares in the medical industry.</p></p>
