<p><ul><li>Ordinary PCR Amplifier</li><li>Gradient PCR Amplifier</li><li>In situ PCR Amplifier</li><li>Real-time Fluorescence Quantification PCR Amplifier</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCR amplifier market consists of various types of amplifiers used in polymerase chain reaction (PCR) technology. Ordinary PCR amplifiers are commonly used for standard PCR procedures. Gradient PCR amplifiers allow for simultaneous testing of multiple temperature gradients, enabling optimization of PCR conditions. In situ PCR amplifiers are designed specifically for in situ PCR applications, enabling the amplification of DNA directly within biological samples. Real-time fluorescence quantification PCR amplifiers provide real-time monitoring and quantification of PCR reactions, allowing for accurate and efficient DNA analysis.</p></p>
