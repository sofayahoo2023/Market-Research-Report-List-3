<p><p>Neurobiology ELISA Kits are used in the field of neurobiology to detect and quantify specific molecules such as proteins. The market for these kits is categorized into different types based on the method used. Direct ELISA involves directly detecting the target molecule, while Sandwich ELISA detects the target using two antibodies. Indirect ELISA involves using a secondary antibody, and Competitive ELISA measures the competition between the target molecule and a labeled molecule. These different types cater to the diverse needs of researchers in neurobiology.</p></p>
