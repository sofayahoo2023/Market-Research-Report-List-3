<p><strong>The Optical Fingerprint Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multispectral Imaging</li><li>Electro optical Imaging</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical fingerprint sensor market is classified into two main types: multispectral imaging and electro optical imaging. Multispectral imaging utilizes multiple light sources to capture high resolution images of the fingerprint, making it suitable for environments with varying lighting conditions. On the other hand, electro optical imaging uses a single light source to scan the fingerprint, offering a cost-effective solution for basic fingerprint recognition applications. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in biometric security systems by accurately verifying an individual's identity.</p></p>
