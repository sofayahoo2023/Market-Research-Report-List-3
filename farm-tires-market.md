<p><p>Farm tires are categorized into two main market types: Bias Tires and Radial Tires. Bias tires are constructed with overlapping ply layers that run at a 30 to 45 degree angle to the tire's centerline, providing a flexible and durable design suitable for heavy-duty farming operations. Radial tires, on the other hand, feature ply layers that run perpendicular to the tire's centerline, offering improved traction, fuel efficiency, and overall performance. Both types of tires cater to the diverse needs of farmers, with each offering unique advantages for different applications.</p></p>
