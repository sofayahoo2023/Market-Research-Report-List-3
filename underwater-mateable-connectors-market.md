<p><p>Underwater mateable connectors are used in underwater applications for connecting electrical and optical equipment. Dry mate connectors are designed to be mated and unmated in a dry environment, while wet mate connectors can be mated and unmated underwater. The dry mate connector market caters to applications where equipment needs to be connected above water before being submerged, while the wet mate connector market serves applications where connections need to be made underwater without the need for dry mating beforehand.</p></p>
