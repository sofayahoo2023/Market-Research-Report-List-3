<p><p>Planimeters are used to measure the area of an irregular shape by tracing its outline. There are different types of planimeters available in the market. Polar Planimeters measure areas by tracing the boundary of the shape with a rotating wheel. Linear Planimeters measure areas by tracing along a straight line. Prytz Planimeters have a unique design with a rotating disc for area measurement. Other types of planimeters may include digital or electronic versions that offer more accuracy and convenience for measuring areas of various shapes.</p></p>
