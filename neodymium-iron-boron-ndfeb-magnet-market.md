<p><p>Neodymium-Iron-Boron (NdFeB) magnets are categorized into two main types: Sintered NdFeB Magnet and Bonded NdFeB Magnet. Sintered NdFeB magnets are made through a process of sintering fine powder, resulting in high magnetic strength and energy product. On the other hand, Bonded NdFeB magnets are produced by mixing NdFeB powder with a binder material, providing flexibility in shape and size. Both types of NdFeB magnets are widely used in various industries due to their superior magnetic properties and performance.</p></p>
