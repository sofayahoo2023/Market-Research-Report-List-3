<p><strong>The Analog Radiography Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Film Based</li><li>CR Film Cassette</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Analog radiography systems consist of film-based and CR film cassette market types. Film-based systems utilize traditional X-ray film to capture images, which must be developed in a chemical solution. On the other hand, CR film cassette systems use a phosphor plate that captures X-ray images and transfers them to a computer for digital processing. Both market types cater to healthcare facilities looking for cost-effective imaging solutions, with film-based systems being more affordable and widely used in smaller practices, while CR film cassette systems offer the advantages of digital imaging technology.</p></p>
