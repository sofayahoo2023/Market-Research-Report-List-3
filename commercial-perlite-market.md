<p><p>Commercial Perlite is available in two main market types - Crude Form and Expanded Form. Crude Form Perlite is the raw material extracted from mines and typically used in industries like construction for insulation purposes. On the other hand, Expanded Form Perlite is processed to expand its volume and improve its insulation properties, making it suitable for horticultural applications such as soil conditioning and hydroponic growing.
