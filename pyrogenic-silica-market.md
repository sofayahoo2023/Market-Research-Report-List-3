<p><p>Pyrogenic silica, also known as fumed silica, is a high-performance mineral additive used in various industries such as pharmaceuticals, rubber, paints, coatings, adhesives, and sealants. It is produced by burning silicon tetrachloride in a hydrogen-oxygen flame to create very fine silica particles with high surface area and purity. Pyrogenic silica possesses properties such as low density, high purity, large surface area, and excellent thermal stability, making it desirable in a wide range of applications.</p><p>The Pyrogenic Silica Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.20% during the forecast period.
