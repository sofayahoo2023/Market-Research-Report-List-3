<p><ul><li>Constant Climate Chamber</li><li>Dynamic Climate Chamber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Small benchtop climate chambers are commonly divided into two main types: constant climate chambers and dynamic climate chambers. Constant climate chambers are used for steady-state conditions, maintaining a consistent temperature and humidity level for extended periods of time. On the other hand, dynamic climate chambers are capable of creating varying environmental conditions, simulating changing temperatures and humidity levels to replicate real-world conditions. These two types of climate chambers serve different purposes in research, testing, and development across various industries.</p></p>
