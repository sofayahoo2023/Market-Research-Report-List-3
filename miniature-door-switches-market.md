<p><ul><li>Mechanical Switches</li><li>Non-Contact Switches</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Miniature door switches come in two main types: mechanical switches and non-contact switches. Mechanical switches use physical movement to open or close the circuit when the door is opened or closed. On the other hand, non-contact switches use magnetic or infrared technology to detect the proximity of the door without any physical contact. Both types of switches are used in various applications where space is limited, such as in vending machines, appliances, and security systems.</p></p>
