<p><p>The operating instrument set market is divided into two types: disposable and non-disposable. Disposable instrument sets are meant for single use only and are discarded after each procedure. They provide convenience and reduce the risk of cross-contamination. On the other hand, non-disposable instrument sets are designed for multiple uses. They are sterilized and reused after each procedure. Non-disposable sets are more cost-effective in the long run but require rigorous sterilization processes to maintain their safety and efficacy. Both types cater to the varied needs and preferences of healthcare professionals.</p></p>
