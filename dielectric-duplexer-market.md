<p><ul><li>Mono Type</li><li>Array Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dielectric Duplexer Market Types include Mono Type and Array Type. Mono Type Duplexers consist of a single set of filters for both transmitting and receiving signals, offering simpler design and lower cost. Array Type Duplexers utilize multiple sets of filters for transmitting and receiving signals, providing better isolation and higher performance, but are more complex and expensive. Each type has its own advantages and disadvantages, catering to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
