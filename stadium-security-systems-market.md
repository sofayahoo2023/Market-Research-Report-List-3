<p><p>Stadium security systems comprise two main market types, namely Access Control Systems and Video Surveillance Systems. Access Control Systems include technologies like turnstiles, biometric scanners, and ticket validation systems that regulate entry into the stadium. Video Surveillance Systems, on the other hand, utilize cameras strategically placed around the stadium to monitor crowds, identify suspicious activities, and provide real-time footage to security personnel. Both market types aim to enhance security measures and ensure the safety of all individuals within the stadium premises.</p></p>
