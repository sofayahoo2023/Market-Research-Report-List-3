<p><p>Cadmium Selenide market is divided into two main types: Single Crystal and Multi Crystal. Single Crystal Cadmium Selenide refers to a high-purity crystal structure with minimal defects, making it ideal for advanced semiconductor applications. On the other hand, Multi Crystal Cadmium Selenide consists of multiple crystals grown together, offering lower purity levels but higher production yield. Single Crystal is preferred for high-end electronic devices, while Multi Crystal is more commonly used in consumer electronics and solar panels.</p></p>
