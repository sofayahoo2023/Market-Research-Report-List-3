<p><p>Heat sealable BOPP films are categorized based on their thickness into four main types: below 15 micron, 15-30 micron, 30-45 micron, and above 45 micron. Below 15 micron films are typically used for lightweight packaging applications. Films within the 15-30 micron range are versatile and commonly used in a variety of industries. 30-45 micron films are ideal for medium-duty packaging needs. Films above 45 micron are heavy-duty and offer superior strength and durability, making them suitable for demanding packaging requirements.</p></p>
