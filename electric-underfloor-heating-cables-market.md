<p><p>Electric underfloor heating cables come in two main types - single conductor cable and twin conductor cable. Single conductor cables consist of a single heating element surrounded by an insulating jacket, making them easy to install in small or irregularly shaped areas. Twin conductor cables have two heating elements running parallel to each other, providing more even heat distribution and making them suitable for larger, regular-shaped spaces. Both types of cables are popular choices for providing efficient and comfortable heating in residential and commercial buildings.</p></p>
