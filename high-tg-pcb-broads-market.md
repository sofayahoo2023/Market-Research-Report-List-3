<p><p>High-Tg PCB boards are classified into three market types based on their glass transition temperature (Tg). Normal market includes boards with Tg ranges of 130℃, 135℃, and 140℃, suitable for basic applications. Medium market comprises boards with Tg ranges of 150℃ and 155℃, ideal for more complex and high-temperature applications. High market consists of boards with Tg above 170℃, specifically designed for extremely high-temperature environments. Each market type caters to different levels of thermal requirements and application demands.</p></p>
