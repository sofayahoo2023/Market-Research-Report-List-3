<p><strong>The Powder-Actuated Nail Gun Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Drive Nail Gun</li><li>Indirect Drive Nail Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Direct Drive Nail guns are designed with a piston that directly strikes the nail, providing more power and efficiency. These are perfect for heavy-duty projects like framing and decking. Indirect Drive Nail guns, on the other hand, use a striking pin that impacts a separate piston, reducing recoil and allowing for more precise placement. These are commonly used for delicate work like trim carpentry and molding. Both types offer advantages based on the specific needs of the project at hand.</p></p>
