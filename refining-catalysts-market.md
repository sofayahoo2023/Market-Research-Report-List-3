<p><ul><li>FCC Catalysts</li><li>Hydro-processing Catalyst</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refining catalysts are mainly divided into three market types: Fluid Catalytic Cracking (FCC) Catalysts, Hydro-processing Catalysts, and Others. FCC catalysts are used to convert high-molecular-weight hydrocarbons into smaller, more valuable products. Hydro-processing catalysts are used to remove impurities from crude oil and convert heavy hydrocarbons into lighter, more valuable products. The "Others" category includes various catalysts used in different refining processes such as alkylation and isomerization. Each type of catalyst plays a vital role in enhancing the efficiency and productivity of oil refining processes.</p></p>
