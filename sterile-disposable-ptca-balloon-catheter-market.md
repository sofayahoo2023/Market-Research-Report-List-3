<p><p>Sterile Disposable PTCA Balloon Catheter market types include Semi-Compliant, Non-Compliant, and Compliant catheters. Semi-Compliant catheters have moderate flexibility and can expand to a certain extent. Non-Compliant catheters have minimal flexibility and are designed for high-pressure angioplasty. Compliant catheters are highly flexible and can conform to the contours of the blood vessels for a more gentle and controlled dilation. Each type of catheter serves different purposes in percutaneous transluminal coronary angioplasty (PTCA) procedures.</p></p>
