<p><p>Injection moulding solutions market types include hydraulic, all-electric, and hybrid systems. Hydraulic systems utilize hydraulic pumps and valves to operate the machine, providing high force and capacity, but with high energy consumption. All-electric systems rely on electric servo motors, offering high precision, speed, and energy efficiency. Hybrid systems combine hydraulic and electric components, combining the advantages of both systems. These market types cater to different customer requirements and application needs, providing various options for manufacturers in the injection moulding industry.</p></p>
