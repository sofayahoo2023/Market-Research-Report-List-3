<p><strong>The Nanofibrillar Cellulose Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>Nanofibrillar Cellulose market can be classified into three types based on production methods - Acid Hydrolysis Method, Enzymatic Hydrolysis Method, and Electrospinning market. Acid Hydrolysis Method involves breaking down cellulose fibers using strong acids. Enzymatic Hydrolysis Method utilizes enzymes to break down cellulose into nanofibrils. Electrospinning market involves creating nanofibrils by applying an electric field to a solution of cellulose. Each method has its own advantages and applications in various industries such as packaging, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals.</p></p>
