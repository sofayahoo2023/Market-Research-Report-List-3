<p><p>Point of Sale (POS) software is classified into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based POS software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It allows businesses to store and manage sales data securely in the cloud, enabling real-time updates and access from anywhere. On the other hand, on-premise POS software is installed locally on a business's hardware. It does not require an internet connection but limits access to the physical location. Both types have their own advantages, and businesses choose based on their specific requirements and preferences.</p></p>
