<p><p>Liquid fertilizer market can be categorised into four major types based on the essential nutrients they provide to plants: </p><p>1. Potash fertilizers contain high levels of potassium which is crucial for plant growth and development. </p><p>2. Micronutrient fertilizers provide essential micronutrients like iron, zinc, and copper which are required in small amounts for healthy plant growth. </p><p>3. Phosphorous fertilizers are rich in phosphorus, an essential nutrient for root development and overall plant health. </p><p>4. Nitrogen fertilizers are crucial for promoting green leafy growth and overall plant vigor.</p></p>
