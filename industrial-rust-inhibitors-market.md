<p><p>Industrial Rust Inhibitors are products used to protect metal surfaces from corrosion. There are two main types in the market - Solvent-based and Water-based Industrial Rust Inhibitors. Solvent-based inhibitors typically contain oil-based carriers that provide a protective coating on the metal surface. On the other hand, Water-based inhibitors use water as a carrier which evaporates, leaving behind a protective film on the metal surface. Both types offer effective protection against rust and corrosion, with each having its own set of advantages and applications in various industries.</p></p>
