<p><strong>The Digital Differential Pressure Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Silicon Based</li><li>Foil Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital differential pressure sensors can be classified into two main types: Silicon Based and Foil Based. Silicon-based sensors utilize a silicon diaphragm to measure pressure differentials, offering high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, foil-based sensors use a thin metal diaphragm, providing increased sensitivity and response time. Both types of sensors are widely used in various industrial and commercial applications where precise pressure measurements are required, such as HVAC systems, medical devices, and automotive applications.</p></p>
