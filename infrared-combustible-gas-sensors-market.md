<p><p>Infrared combustible gas sensors are used for detecting the presence of flammable gases in various industries. Two main types of these sensors include point detectors and open path detectors. </p><p>Point detectors are stationary devices that are placed at specific locations to monitor gas levels in a confined space. </p><p>Open path detectors use beams of infrared light to detect gases across a larger area, making them ideal for applications where gas leaks may occur over a wider area. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring safety and preventing accidents in industrial settings.</p></p>
