<p><p>Mineral UV filters for personal care can be categorized into two types based on their composition in formulations: water phase and oil phase filters. Water phase filters are soluble in water and are often used in lightweight and non-greasy sunscreen formulations. In contrast, oil phase filters are oil-soluble and are commonly found in products like sunscreens and moisturizers that provide a more nourishing and moisturizing feel on the skin. Both types of filters offer effective protection against UV radiation and are essential components in sun protection products.</p></p>
