<p><ul><li>Dry-type Air Filter</li><li>Wet-type Air Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Air Filter Market offers two main types of filters: Dry-type and Wet-type Air Filters. Dry-type filters are made of pleated paper or synthetic materials, allowing air to pass through while capturing particles. They are commonly used in commercial vehicles for their efficiency and easy maintenance. On the other hand, Wet-type filters use oil to trap and remove contaminants from the air. They provide better filtration but require regular maintenance and oil replenishment. Both types cater to the specific needs and preferences of commercial vehicle owners.</p></p>
