<p><strong>The Urinary Self-Catheter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Foley Catheters</li><li>Intermittent Catheters</li><li>External Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The urinary self-catheter market consists of three main types, including Foley catheters, intermittent catheters, and external catheters. Foley catheters are indwelling catheters that remain in the bladder for a longer period of time. Intermittent catheters are used to drain the bladder periodically and are inserted and removed as needed. External catheters are worn externally, typically by male patients, to collect urine. Each type of catheter serves a specific purpose in managing urinary retention or incontinence, catering to different patient needs.</p></p>
