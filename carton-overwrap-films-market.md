<p><p>Carton overwrap films are widely used in the packaging industry. Shrink films are a type of carton overwrap film that, when heated, shrink tightly around the product, providing secure and tamper-resistant packaging. They are commonly used for packaging food and beverages. On the other hand, stretch films are another type of carton overwrap film that is elastic and can be stretched to tightly wrap around the product. These films are commonly used for bundling and securing palletized goods during transportation and storage. Both shrink films and stretch films provide excellent protection and are cost-effective packaging solutions.</p></p>
