<p><p>Custom blend food colors are available in three main types: powder, liquid, and gel. Powder colors are dry and easily mix into dry ingredients, making them ideal for products like cake mixes. Liquid colors are more concentrated and are best for coloring large amounts of liquid or moist ingredients. Gel colors are thicker and more concentrated than liquid colors, making them suitable for coloring icings and frostings. Each type of color has its own unique advantages and is used in different types of food applications to achieve desired color effects.</p></p>
