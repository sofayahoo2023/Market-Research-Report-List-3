<p><p>CPA liability insurance market types include Directors and Officers (D&O) Insurance and Errors and Omissions (E&O) Insurance. D&O insurance provides coverage for claims made against company directors and officers for alleged wrongful acts. E&O insurance, on the other hand, covers claims of negligence or inadequate work performance. Both types of insurance are essential for CPAs to protect themselves and their businesses from financial losses due to legal challenges.
