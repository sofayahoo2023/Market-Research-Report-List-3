<p><strong>The Engineered Flooring Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Three Layers Type</li><li>Multilayer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engineered flooring market consists of two main types: Three Layers Type and Multilayer Type. Three Layers Type is made up of three layers - a top layer of real hardwood, a core layer of plywood or fiberboard, and a bottom layer of hardwood or softwood. This type offers durability and stability. On the other hand, Multilayer Type is made up of multiple thin layers of wood pressed together, providing a strong and stable flooring option. Both types offer a wide range of styles and finishes to suit different design preferences.</p></p>
