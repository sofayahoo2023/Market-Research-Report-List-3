<p><p>Steering Tie Rod Market consists of two main types: Inner Tie Rod and Outer Tie Rod. Inner Tie Rod connects the steering rack to the outer tie rod end, providing smooth and accurate steering input. Outer Tie Rod connects the inner tie rod to the steering knuckle, allowing for the turning of the wheels. Both types play crucial roles in steering control and alignment of the vehicle, ensuring safe and efficient driving performance. These components are essential for the proper functioning of the steering system in vehicles.</p></p>
