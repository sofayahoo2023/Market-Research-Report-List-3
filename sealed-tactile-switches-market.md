<p><strong>The Sealed Tactile Switches Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface-Mounting</li><li>Throgh-hole Mounting</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sealed tactile switches come in two main market types: surface-mounting and through-hole mounting. Surface-mounting switches are soldered directly onto the surface of a printed circuit board, making them more compact and suitable for dense designs. Through-hole mounting switches are inserted through holes in the PCB and soldered from the other side, providing a stronger mechanical connection. Both types offer the benefit of sealed construction, protecting the internal components from dust, moisture, and other environmental factors.</p></p>
