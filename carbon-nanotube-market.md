<p><p>Carbon Nanotube Market consists of three main types: Single-Wall Nanotubes (SWNT), Multi-Wall Nanotubes (MWNT), and Double-Wall Nanotubes (DWNT). SWNTs are composed of a single layer of carbon atoms, MWNTs consist of multiple layers of carbon atoms arranged concentrically, and DWNTs are a hybrid of SWNTs and MWNTs with two layers of carbon atoms. Each type has unique properties and applications, making them valuable in various industries such as electronics, aerospace, and material science.</p></p>
