<p><p>Internet Data Centers (IDCs) can be classified into different market types based on their structure and setup. Shared Facility Centers are used by multiple tenants, while Stand-alone Centers cater to a single tenant. Modular Centers consist of prefabricated modules that can be rapidly deployed, Pre-Built Centers are pre-designed and ready for immediate use, and Mobile Data Centers are transportable units that can be quickly relocated. Each market type offers unique advantages and caters to different business needs in the realm of data storage and management.</p></p>
