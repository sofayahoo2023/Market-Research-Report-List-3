<p><p>Radiographic testing market includes various types such as Film Radiography, Real Time Radiography (RTR), Computed Tomography (CT), Digital Radiography (DR), and Computed Radiography (CR). Film Radiography involves capturing images on film, RTR provides instant radiographic images, CT offers 3D imaging, DR uses digital sensors for image capture, and CR utilizes phosphor plates for image recording. Each type has its unique advantages in terms of image quality, speed, and cost, catering to different needs in the radiographic testing industry.</p></p>
