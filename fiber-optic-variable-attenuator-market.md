<p><ul><li>MEMS Variable Fiber Attenuator</li><li>High Speed Variable Fiber Attenuator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fiber optic variable attenuators are available in two main types: MEMS variable fiber attenuators and High Speed variable fiber attenuators. MEMS variable attenuators utilize microelectromechanical systems technology to control the level of attenuation in optical fiber networks. On the other hand, High Speed variable attenuators are designed to operate at high speeds, making them suitable for applications where rapid adjustments in signal intensity are required. Both types offer precision control over the attenuation levels in fiber optic networks, catering to different requirements in the market.</p></p>
