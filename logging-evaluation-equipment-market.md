<p><strong>The Logging Evaluation Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wireline Logging Evaluation</li><li>Formation Evaluation while Drilling (FEWD)</li><li>Surface Logging Service (SLS)</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Logging evaluation equipment market can be divided into four main types:</p><p>1. Wireline Logging Evaluation: This involves lowering a tool down a wellbore to measure properties of the surrounding rock formations.</p><p>2. Formation Evaluation while Drilling (FEWD): This type of evaluation is done in real-time while drilling is taking place, providing immediate data on formation properties.</p><p>3. Surface Logging Service (SLS): This involves analyzing data collected at the surface during drilling operations.</p><p>4. Other market: This category includes specialized logging evaluation equipment and technologies that do not fall into the above three types.</p></p>
