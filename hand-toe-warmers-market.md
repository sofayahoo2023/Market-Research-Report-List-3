<p><p>Hand & toe warmers are available in various types such as air activated (iron), supersaturated solution (crystallisation-type), lighter fuel, battery-operated, and other forms. Air activated warmers work by exposing iron powder to air, producing heat. Supersaturated solution warmers generate heat through the crystallisation process. Lighter fuel warmers use a flammable liquid to generate heat. Battery-operated warmers use electricity to produce heat. There are also other forms of hand & toe warmers available on the market that utilize different heating mechanisms.</p></p>
