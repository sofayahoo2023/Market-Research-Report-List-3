<p><p>Aramid fibers are divided into three main types: Para-Aramid, Meta-Aramid, and others. Para-Aramid fibers, such as Kevlar, are known for their exceptional strength and heat resistance, making them ideal for applications requiring high performance. Meta-Aramid fibers, like Nomex, are valued for their flame resistance and thermal stability, commonly used in protective clothing and industrial applications. Other types of aramid fibers, including copolyamides and polyimides, offer a range of properties suitable for specific applications such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
