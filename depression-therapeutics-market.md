<p><p>Depression therapeutics market is categorized into various types, including Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitors (SSRIs), Selective Norepinephrine Reuptake Inhibitors (SNRIs), and Others. SSRIs work by increasing serotonin levels in the brain, improving mood and reducing symptoms of depression. SNRIs, on the other hand, target both serotonin and norepinephrine, providing relief for those who may not respond to SSRIs alone. The Others category includes various medications and treatments that aim to address depression through different mechanisms.</p></p>
