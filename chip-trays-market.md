<p><p>The chip trays market has various types such as MPPE, PES, PS, ABS, and others. MPPE stands for Modified Polyphenylene Ether, known for its high heat resistance. PES, or Polyethersulfone, offers excellent chemical resistance. PS, or Polystyrene, is known for its clarity and rigidity. ABS, or Acrylonitrile Butadiene Styrene, is valued for its toughness and impact resistance. Other materials used in chip trays include PET, PVC, and polycarbonate, each offering unique properties suitable for different applications in the market.</p></p>
