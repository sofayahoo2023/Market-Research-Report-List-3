<p><strong>The Automotive Distributor Caps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Standard Socket Distributor Caps</li><li>Post Type HEI Distributor Caps</li><li>Recessed Pin Distributor Caps</li><li>External Pin Post Distributor Caps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive distributor caps come in various types to suit different ignition systems. Standard socket distributor caps feature a central socket for the spark plug wire connections. Post type HEI distributor caps have a single post in the center for connecting the rotor. Recessed pin distributor caps have pins that are recessed into the cap for a secure connection. External pin post distributor caps have pins on the outside of the cap for easy installation. Each type caters to specific ignition system requirements in the automotive industry.</p></p>
