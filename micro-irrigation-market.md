<p><p>Micro Irrigation Market Types refer to different irrigation systems designed to efficiently deliver water directly to the roots of plants in agriculture and landscaping. Porous Soaker Hose System releases water slowly along the length of the hose. Emitter Drip System uses emitters to deliver water directly to the base of plants. Watermatic Drip System involves a network of pipes with holes to distribute water. Micro Misting Sprinkler system releases fine droplets of water for delicate plants. These systems help conserve water and improve plant growth.</p></p>
