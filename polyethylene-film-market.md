<p><p>Polyethylene film market types can be categorized into High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE is known for its high strength and durability, making it ideal for applications requiring toughness and resistance to chemicals. LDPE, on the other hand, has a lower density and is more flexible, making it suitable for applications where conformability and impact resistance are desired. Both types of polyethylene films have their unique advantages and are used in a wide range of industries for packaging, construction, agriculture, and more.</p></p>
