<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cargo tank coatings are typically divided into two main types: water-based and solvent-based. Water-based coatings use water as a carrier for the coating resins, making them eco-friendly and easy to clean up. Solvent-based coatings, on the other hand, use chemical solvents as carriers, providing better adhesion and durability. These two types cater to different preferences and requirements in the market, with water-based coatings being favored for their environmental benefits and solvent-based coatings for their performance advantages.</p></p>
