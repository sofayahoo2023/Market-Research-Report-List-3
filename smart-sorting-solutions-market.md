<p><ul><li>Linear Sorting Systems</li><li>Loop Sorting Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The smart sorting solutions market comprises two main types: linear sorting systems and loop sorting systems. Linear sorting systems consist of a straight conveyor belt on which items are sorted based on predefined criteria. Loop sorting systems, on the other hand, involve a continuous loop conveyor belt that allows items to be sorted multiple times according to different criteria. Both types of systems are used in industries such as logistics, postal services, and e-commerce to automate and optimize the sorting process, improving efficiency and accuracy.</p></p>
