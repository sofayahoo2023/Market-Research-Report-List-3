<p><p>A Flexible AC Transmission System (FACTS) is a set of power electronic devices used to enhance the controllability and flexibility of AC transmission systems. The two main types are Shunt Compensation, which involves injecting or absorbing reactive power at specific locations to regulate voltage levels, and Series Compensation, which involves inserting reactance in series with the transmission line to improve power transfer capability. Both types of FACTS devices help improve the efficiency and reliability of power transmission systems.</p></p>
