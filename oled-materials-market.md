<p><strong>The OLED Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Emitting Layer (EML)</li><li>Hole Transport Layer (HTL)</li><li>Electron Transport Layer (ETL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>OLED materials market comprises different types of layers essential for OLED displays. The Emitting Layer (EML) is responsible for emitting light when an electric current passes through it. The Hole Transport Layer (HTL) helps in transporting positive charges, while the Electron Transport Layer (ETL) facilitates the movement of negative charges within the OLED structure. These materials are crucial for the efficient functioning of OLED displays and play a significant role in determining the overall performance and quality of the display.</p></p>
