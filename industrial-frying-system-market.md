<p><ul><li>Batch</li><li>Continuous</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Industrial frying systems can be categorized into two main types: batch frying systems and continuous frying systems. Batch frying systems involve frying a set amount of product in a single batch, making them ideal for small-scale or specialty production. On the other hand, continuous frying systems are designed for large-scale and high-volume production, allowing for a continuous flow of product through the fryer. Each system offers its own unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs and requirements of the manufacturer.</p></p>
