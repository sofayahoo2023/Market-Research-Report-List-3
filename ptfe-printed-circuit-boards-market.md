<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PTFE Printed Circuit Boards market is divided into two main types: Single Layer and Multi-layer boards. Single Layer boards consist of a single layer of substrate with conductive traces on one side, making them suitable for simpler designs and low-cost applications. In contrast, Multi-layer boards have multiple layers of substrate with conductive traces, allowing for more complex designs and enhanced functionality. Multi-layer boards are typically used in high-performance electronic devices that require dense circuitry and signal integrity.</p></p>
