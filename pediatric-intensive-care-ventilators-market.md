<p><ul><li>Invasive</li><li>Non-Invasive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Pediatric Intensive Care Ventilators market includes two types, invasive and non-invasive. Invasive ventilators are used when a child cannot breathe on their own, and a tube is inserted into their airway. These ventilators deliver breaths directly into the lungs. On the other hand, non-invasive ventilators are used for children who can breathe on their own but need assistance. These devices provide positive pressure through a mask or nasal prongs, helping to keep the airways open and supporting breathing efforts.</p></p>
