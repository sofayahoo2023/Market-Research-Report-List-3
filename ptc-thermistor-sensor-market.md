<p><p>PTC Thermistor Sensor market consists of two main types - CPTC (Conductive PTC) and PPTC (Polymer PTC). CPTC sensors exhibit an increase in resistance as temperature rises, while PPTC sensors show a decrease in resistance. CPTC sensors are commonly used in applications where overcurrent protection is required, such as in power supplies and batteries. On the other hand, PPTC sensors are widely used in consumer electronics and automotive industry for temperature sensing and overcurrent protection. Both types offer reliable and efficient temperature sensing solutions in various industries.</p></p>
