<p><p>Buck-boost regulators are DC-DC converters that can step up or step down the input voltage to provide a stable output voltage. The market types for buck-boost regulators include integrated switch, external switch, and integrated inductor. Integrated switch regulators combine the switch, control circuitry, and possibly other components in a single package. External switch regulators require an external switch to operate. Integrated inductor regulators have the inductor integrated into the same package. Each market type offers different advantages in terms of efficiency, size, and cost.</p></p>
