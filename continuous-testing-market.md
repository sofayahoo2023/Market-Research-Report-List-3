<p><p>Continuous testing is a software testing method that involves the execution of automated tests throughout the software development lifecycle (SDLC) to provide immediate feedback on the business risks associated with software changes. These methodologies promote faster and more frequent releases, necessitating the need for continuous testing to maintain quality and ensure timely delivery. Additionally, the rising demand for cloud-based testing solutions and growing awareness about the benefits of continuous testing among enterprises are driving market growth.</p><p>Furthermore, the increasing complexity of software applications, especially with the proliferation of mobile and web-based applications, has created a demand for continuous testing solutions.
