It is commonly used in bridges, buildings, parking structures, and other types of infrastructure to increase the strength and durability of the concrete. <p><p>Prestressed concrete strand market consists of various types including bare PC strand, grease filled PC strand, wax filled PC strand, and others. Bare PC strand is used in applications where corrosion protection is not required. Grease filled PC strand is treated with a special grease coating for improved corrosion resistance. Each type offers different features and benefits for construction purposes.</p></p>
