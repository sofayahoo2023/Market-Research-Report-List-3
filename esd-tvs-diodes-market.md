<p><strong>The ESD TVS Diodes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Junction Capacitive Type</li><li>Low Junction Capacitive Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>ESD TVS diodes are classified into two main types based on junction capacitance: High Junction Capacitive Type and Low Junction Capacitive Type. </p><p>High Junction Capacitive Type diodes have a higher junction capacitance, which allows them to provide better ESD protection but may result in slower response times. On the other hand, Low Junction Capacitive Type diodes have lower junction capacitance, making them ideal for high-speed applications where fast response times are critical. Manufacturers choose between these types based on their specific needs for ESD protection and signal integrity.</p></p>
