<p><p>The graphic processors market is divided into two main types: discrete type and integrated type. Discrete graphic processors are separate units dedicated solely to graphics processing, offering higher performance and more customization options but requiring additional space and power. Integrated graphic processors are integrated into the main central processing unit, providing lower performance but requiring less power and space. Both types cater to different segments of users depending on their requirements for graphics processing power and efficiency.</p></p>
