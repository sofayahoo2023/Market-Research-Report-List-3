<p><p>Subsea pipeline jumpers are used to connect pipelines, risers, and manifolds in offshore oil and gas installations. There are two main types of jumpers available in the market - rigid pipeline jumpers and flexible pipeline jumpers. Rigid pipeline jumpers are made of solid materials like steel and are less flexible but provide more structural support. Flexible pipeline jumpers are made of materials like flexible pipes or hoses, allowing them to bend and adapt to changes in seabed conditions, offering greater versatility in subsea installations.</p></p>
