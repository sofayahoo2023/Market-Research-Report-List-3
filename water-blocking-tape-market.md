<p><p>Water blocking tape is a crucial component in cable manufacturing to prevent water ingress. There are three types of water blocking tapes available in the market: non-conductive type, semi-conductive type, and conductive type. Non-conductive type tapes are made of non-conductive materials and provide a barrier against water. Semi-conductive type tapes have some conductivity to dissipate static charges. Conductive type tapes are made of conductive materials to provide maximum protection against water and electrical currents. Each type serves a specific purpose based on the cable's requirements.</p></p>
