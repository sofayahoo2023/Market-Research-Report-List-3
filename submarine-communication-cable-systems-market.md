<p><p>The submarine communication cable systems market consists of two types: Single Core Cable Systems and Multicore Cable Systems. Single Core Cable Systems involve the use of a single fiber optic cable to transmit data and information signals underwater. Both types play a crucial role in establishing reliable and high-speed communication networks across oceans and seas.</p></p>
