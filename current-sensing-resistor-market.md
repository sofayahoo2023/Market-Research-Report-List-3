<p><p>Current sensing resistors are mainly classified into three types - metal resistor, film resistor, and others. Metal resistors are made of metal wire wound around a ceramic core, offering high power rating and high precision. Film resistors use a thin film of metal or carbon on a ceramic substrate, providing a stable and reliable performance. Other types of current sensing resistors include carbon composition resistors and thick film resistors, each with their own unique characteristics and applications in different electronic circuits.</p></p>
