<p><ul><li>Ag Paste</li><li>Al Paste</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solar paste market is typically segmented into two major types: Ag Paste and Al Paste. Ag Paste, also known as silver paste, is commonly used for the front side metallization of solar cells due to its high electrical conductivity and adhesion properties. On the other hand, Al Paste, or aluminum paste, is used for the backside metallization of solar cells. Both types of pastes play a crucial role in the efficiency and performance of solar cells by facilitating the flow of electricity within the cell structure.</p></p>
