<p><p>Lead frames are critical components in the semiconductor industry, with two main types: stamping process lead frames and etching process lead frames. Stamping process lead frames are manufactured by stamping metal sheets to create the desired shape and design. On the other hand, etching process lead frames are produced by chemically etching metal sheets to create intricate patterns and designs. Both types of lead frames play a crucial role in providing structural support and electrical connectivity for semiconductor devices.</p></p>
