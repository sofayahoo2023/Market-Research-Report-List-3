<p><p>Natural gas-fired electricity generation can be categorized into two main types of markets: Combined Cycle Gas Turbine (CCGT) and Open Cycle Gas Turbine (OCGT). CCGT plants are more efficient and commonly used for base load electricity generation due to their higher efficiency in converting natural gas into electricity. On the other hand, OCGT plants are more flexible and typically used for peak load electricity generation, providing quick response times during periods of high demand. Both types play a crucial role in meeting the varying electricity needs of a region.</p></p>
