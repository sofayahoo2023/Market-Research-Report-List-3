Increasing awareness about the importance of treating sleep apnea and the availability of reimbursement programs for CPAP therapy further contribute to market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the CPAP masks market is the development of smart masks. <p><p>The Continuous Positive Airway Pressure (CPAP) masks market includes three main types: nasal pillow masks, nasal masks, and full face masks. Nasal pillow masks are the least invasive and feature small pillows that fit directly into the nostrils. Nasal masks cover the nose and offer a secure fit, while full face masks cover both the nose and mouth for individuals who breathe through their mouth or experience higher pressures. All three types help deliver continuous air pressure to individuals with sleep apnea to ensure uninterrupted breathing during sleep.</p></p>
