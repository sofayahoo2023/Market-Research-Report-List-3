<p><p>The High Performance MEMS Inertial Sensor market includes three main types: MEMS Acceleration Sensor, MEMS Gyroscope, and MEMS Inertial Measurement Unit (IMU). MEMS Acceleration Sensors are used to measure changes in velocity and acceleration. MEMS Gyroscopes measure angular velocity and rotational motion. MEMS IMUs combine both acceleration and gyroscope sensors to provide accurate measurements of linear acceleration, angular rate, and sometimes magnetic field data. These sensors are used in a wide range of applications such as automotive, consumer electronics, aerospace, and industrial sectors.</p></p>
