<p><p>Rotary electric ovens come in three main types: vertical type, horizontal type, and others. Vertical type ovens are designed with a rotating carousel inside, allowing for even cooking of multiple items at once. Horizontal type ovens feature a rotating tray system that moves items through the cooking chamber, ensuring uniform cooking. The "others" category includes unique designs that may combine features of vertical and horizontal ovens or offer specialized functions. Each type caters to different cooking needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
