<p><ul><li>Flat Tappet</li><li>Roller Tappet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine tappets are components that transmit motion from the camshaft to the valves in an engine. There are two main types of engine tappets: flat tappets and roller tappets. Flat tappets have a flat surface that slides against the camshaft, while roller tappets have a roller bearing that reduces friction and wear. The market for engine tappets is divided between these two types, with roller tappets being more expensive but offering better performance and durability compared to flat tappets.</p></p>
