<p><p>Deep Learning Chipset market can be categorized into several types based on their design and functionality. Graphics Processing Units (GPUs) are widely used for parallel processing tasks in deep learning applications. Central Processing Units (CPUs) are general-purpose processors used for a variety of tasks. Application Specific Integrated Circuits (ASICs) are custom-designed for specific applications, providing high performance and efficiency. Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) offer flexibility and reconfigurability for different deep learning tasks. Other types of chipsets include specialized processors designed for specific deep learning tasks.</p></p>
