<p><p>Solid Polybutadiene Rubber is classified into different types based on its polymer structure. High Cis Solid Polybutadiene Rubber has a high percentage of cis-1,4 polymer chains, providing excellent elasticity and abrasion resistance. Low Cis Solid Polybutadiene Rubber has a lower percentage of cis-1,4 polymer chains, resulting in improved processability and lower hysteresis. High Trans Solid Polybutadiene Rubber has a high percentage of trans-1,4 polymer chains, offering enhanced tensile strength and resilience. High Vinyl Solid Polybutadiene Rubber has a high percentage of vinyl groups, increasing its compatibility with other materials.</p></p>
