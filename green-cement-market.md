<p><p>Green Cement Market Types refer to different categories of environmentally friendly cement products. Fly-Ash Based Green Cement utilizes fly ash, a byproduct of coal power plants, as a key ingredient. Slag Based Green Cement incorporates slag, a byproduct of steel production. Recycled Aggregates Green Cement utilizes recycled materials such as crushed concrete or glass. Other types include geopolymers and calcium sulfoaluminate cement. These alternatives to traditional Portland cement help reduce carbon footprint and promote sustainability in the construction industry.</p></p>
