<p><p>Fiber Optical Switches are categorized into two main types based on the type of fiber used: Single-mode Fiber Optical Switches and Multimode Fiber Optical Switches. Single-mode switches are designed to work with long-distance, single-mode fibers that transmit data over greater distances with higher speed and lower loss. Multimode switches are used with shorter-distance, multimode fibers that are better suited for shorter data transmissions within a confined area. Both types cater to different network requirements and applications in the market.</p></p>
