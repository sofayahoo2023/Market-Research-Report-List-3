Cement is a key component providing the binding properties. Admixtures are used to improve workability and durability. Fibers are added to increase strength and prevent cracking. Aggregates are used for volume and stability. Additions like fly ash or silica fume enhance properties. Other components such as superplasticizers, viscosity modifiers, or air entraining agents are also utilized in powder type self-compacting concrete production.</p></p>
