<p><p>Textile-based pH controllers consist of two main types: Acid Donor and Alkali Donor markets. Acid Donor pH controllers work by releasing acid into the system to lower pH levels, while Alkali Donor pH controllers release alkali to increase pH levels. These controllers are commonly used in textile industry processes to maintain specific pH levels during dyeing, finishing, and other chemical treatments. Acid Donor controllers are utilized when pH levels need to be lowered, while Alkali Donor controllers are used when pH levels need to be raised.</p></p>
