<p><p>Vehicle Turbocharger Market Types include Mechanical Turbocharging and Exhaust Turbocharging. Mechanical Turbocharging utilizes a turbine connected to the engine's crankshaft to compress air and increase engine power. On the other hand, Exhaust Turbocharging uses exhaust gases to drive a turbine which compresses air for the engine, achieving better fuel efficiency and performance. Both types aim to increase the power and efficiency of vehicles by providing higher air intake pressure for combustion, leading to improved overall performance.</p></p>
