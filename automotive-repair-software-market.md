<p><p>Automotive repair software market can be categorized into two main types: premise-based and cloud-based. Premise-based software is installed and maintained on the user's hardware and servers, requiring manual updates and backups. It provides users with full control over their data but can be more costly and less flexible. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through a web browser, offering flexibility and scalability. It eliminates the need for local installations and allows for automatic updates and data backups, at the cost of potential security concerns.</p></p>
