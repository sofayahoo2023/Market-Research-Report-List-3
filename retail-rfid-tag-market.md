<p><p>Retail RFID tag market consists of three main types: Active RFID tags, which have a built-in power source and can send signals over longer distances; Passive RFID tags, which rely on the reader's signal for power and have shorter read ranges; and Semi-active RFID tags, which have a battery for powering certain functions but still rely on external signals for communication. These different types cater to various use cases and requirements within the retail industry, offering options for tracking inventory, enhancing security, and improving efficiency.</p></p>
