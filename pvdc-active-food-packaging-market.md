<p><strong>The PVDC Active Food Packaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PVDC Latex</li><li>PVDC Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PVDC Active Food Packaging has two main types: PVDC Latex and PVDC Resin. PVDC Latex is a water-based solution used for coating food packaging materials to provide barrier protection against moisture and oxygen. On the other hand, PVDC Resin is a solid form of PVDC that is used in the manufacturing of films and coatings for food packaging. Both types of PVDC are popular choices for active food packaging due to their ability to extend the shelf life of perishable products.</p></p>
