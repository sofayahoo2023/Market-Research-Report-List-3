<p><p>Medical pressure sensors market comprises two main types: MEMS based medical pressure sensors and pressure transducers for medical applications. MEMS based sensors offer high accuracy and sensitivity in measuring pressure within the body, making them ideal for applications such as blood pressure monitoring and respiratory devices. Pressure transducers, on the other hand, are used for broader medical purposes such as monitoring fluid pressure in catheters, infusion pumps, and ventilators. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring accurate and reliable patient monitoring in healthcare settings.</p></p>
