<p><p>Non-contact linear position sensors with analog output signals provide continuous and linear measurement data, allowing for precise positioning information. These sensors are ideal for applications where accuracy is crucial, such as in industrial automation and robotics. On the other hand, sensors with digital output signals provide discrete position data, making them suitable for applications requiring on/off control or simple position detection. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in various industries by enabling efficient and accurate position sensing.</p></p>
