<p><p>The liquid-based needle-free injector market encompasses different types of injectors: jet, spring, laser, and vibration. Jet injectors use high-pressure liquid streams to penetrate the skin and deliver medication. Spring injectors utilize a spring mechanism to force a small needle into the skin. Laser injectors use laser pulses to create microchannels to administer drugs. Vibration injectors utilize high-frequency vibrations to propel liquid medications into the skin. These various types offer alternatives to traditional needle injections, providing painless and efficient drug delivery methods.</p></p>
