<p><p>Industrial sludge treatment chemicals are used to treat and remove sludge from various industrial processes. The market for these chemicals includes flocculants, coagulants, disinfectants, defoamers, and others. Flocculants help in aggregating suspended particles for easier removal, while coagulants aid in forming larger particles for filtration. Disinfectants are used to kill harmful microorganisms in the sludge, while defoamers help in reducing foam formation during treatment. Other chemicals could include pH adjusters, oxidizing agents, and odor control agents.</p></p>
