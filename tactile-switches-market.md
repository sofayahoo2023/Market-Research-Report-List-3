<p><p>Tactile switches are categorized into various types based on their design and functionalities. Standard types are the most common and widely used tactile switches. Illuminated types feature LEDs for backlighting. Sealed types are designed to prevent dust and moisture from entering the switch. SMD types are surface mount devices that are soldered directly onto the PCB. Other types include custom-designed switches for specific applications. Each type cater to different requirements and preferences in the market.</p></p>
