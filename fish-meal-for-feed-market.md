<p><p>Fish meal for feed market can be categorized into three main types based on the fat content: whole fish meal, semi-skimmed fish meal, and defatted fish meal. Whole fish meal contains the highest fat content, providing a good source of energy for animals. Semi-skimmed fish meal has some of the fat removed, making it a balance between energy and protein content. Defatted fish meal has the lowest fat content, being high in protein. Each type caters to different nutritional needs and requirements of livestock and aquaculture animals.</p></p>
