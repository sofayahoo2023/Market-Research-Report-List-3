Double chamber carburizing furnaces have two separate chambers for processing multiple loads simultaneously, increasing productivity. Multi chamber carburizing furnaces feature three or more chambers, offering even higher throughput for large-scale industrial applications. Each type caters to different production needs and capacities.</p></p>
