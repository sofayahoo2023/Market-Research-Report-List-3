They are typically used in substations where the fault current levels are high. On the other hand, High Impedance High Voltage Busbar Protection Devices are designed for busbars with high impedance characteristics, providing reliable protection against faults in systems with lower fault current levels. Both types of protection devices play a critical role in ensuring the safety and reliability of high voltage systems.</p></p>
