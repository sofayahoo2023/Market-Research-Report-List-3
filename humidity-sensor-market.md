<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Humidity Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Humidity Sensor</li><li>Absolute Humidity Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Relative Humidity Sensor measures the amount of water vapor present in the air relative to the maximum amount the air can hold at a specific temperature. It is widely used in weather stations, HVAC systems, and industrial applications. Absolute Humidity Sensor measures the actual amount of water vapor present in the air regardless of temperature. It is commonly used in medical devices, food processing, and environmental monitoring. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in ensuring comfort, safety, and efficiency in various industries.</p></p>
