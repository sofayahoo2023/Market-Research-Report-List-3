<p><strong>The In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Blow IML Containers</li><li>Injection IML Containers</li><li>Thermoforming IML Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>In-Mold Labeled (IML) Containers come in three main types: Blow IML Containers, Injection IML Containers, and Thermoforming IML Containers. Blow IML Containers are created by blow molding the container and then adding the decoration during the molding process. Injection IML Containers involve injecting molten plastic into a mold with the label already inside, resulting in a seamless design. Thermoforming IML Containers are produced by heating a sheet of plastic and then forming it into a container with the label integrated during the process.</p></p>
