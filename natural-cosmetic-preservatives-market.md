<p><strong>The Natural Cosmetic Preservatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lipids</li><li>Acids</li><li>Alcohols</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Natural cosmetic preservatives can be categorized into various types based on their chemical composition and mechanism of action. Lipids, such as oils and fats, are commonly used as preservatives due to their ability to create a barrier that prevents microbial growth. Acids, like citric acid and benzoic acid, lower the pH of the product, making it inhospitable for bacteria. Alcohols, such as ethanol and propanol, disrupt the cell membranes of microorganisms. Other natural preservatives include plant extracts, essential oils, and antimicrobial peptides.</p></p>
