<p><p>Display panels come in various types, with two popular options being LED and OLED. LED panels utilize light-emitting diodes to produce images, offering high brightness and energy efficiency. On the other hand, OLED panels use organic compounds to emit light individually, providing superior color accuracy and contrast. OLED panels are known for their thinness and flexibility, making them ideal for curved or foldable displays. Both LED and OLED panels are widely used in televisions, smartphones, and other electronic devices.</p></p>
