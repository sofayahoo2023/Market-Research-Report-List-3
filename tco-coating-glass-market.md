<p><ul><li>ITO (Indium Tin Oxide)</li><li>FTO (Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide)</li><li>AZO (Aluminum Doped Zinc Oxide)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>TCO coating glass market types include ITO (Indium Tin Oxide), FTO (Fluorine Doped Tin Oxide), and AZO (Aluminum Doped Zinc Oxide). ITO is commonly used for its high electrical conductivity and transparency, making it suitable for touchscreens and displays. FTO offers improved chemical stability and is used in solar panels and antireflection coatings. AZO provides a cost-effective alternative with similar properties to ITO, making it ideal for applications in photovoltaic devices and optoelectronics.</p></p>
