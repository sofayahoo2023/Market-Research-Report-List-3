<p><p>Brake lining market can be categorized into three main types: Asbestos Type, Semimetal Type, and NAO (Non-Asbestos Organic) Type. Asbestos Type brake linings were commonly used in the past but have now been replaced by safer alternatives due to health concerns. Semimetal Type linings are made of a mix of metal and non-metallic materials, providing good performance and durability. NAO Type linings are composed of organic materials such as glass, rubber, and Kevlar, offering good braking efficiency without the health risks associated with asbestos.</p></p>
