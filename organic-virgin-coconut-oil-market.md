<p><p>Organic Virgin Coconut Oil is available in different market types such as Cold Pressed and Expeller-pressed. Cold Pressed coconut oil is extracted by pressing the fresh coconut meat at low temperatures, retaining most of its nutrients and flavor. On the other hand, Expeller-pressed coconut oil is extracted by a mechanical process without using chemicals or heat, making it a less expensive option. Both types of oils are popular in the market for their health benefits and versatility in cooking and beauty products.</p></p>
