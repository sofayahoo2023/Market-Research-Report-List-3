<p><ul><li>Monolithic Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Tank Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Monolithic cleaning equipment is a type of semiconductor cleaning device that is designed for cleaning individual wafers or semiconductor chips. It is typically used for precision cleaning of small components in the semiconductor industry. On the other hand, tank cleaning equipment is used for cleaning larger volumes of semiconductor components or parts in batches. This type of equipment is more suitable for cleaning processes that require immersion or spraying of cleaning solutions on multiple components at once. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in maintaining the cleanliness of semiconductor devices.</p></p>
