<p><p>Thermal interface pads and materials are used to improve heat transfer between heat-generating components and heat sinks in electronic devices. There are three main types of thermal interface materials: thermal grease, which is a viscous substance applied between surfaces to fill gaps and improve thermal conductivity; phase change materials, which change from solid to liquid at a specific temperature to fill gaps; and thermal pads, which are pre-formed pads with good thermal conductivity. Each type has its own advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
