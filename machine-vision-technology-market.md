<p><p>The machine vision technology market can be divided into three main types. Firstly, PC-based machine vision technology uses software programs installed on a personal computer to analyze images from cameras. Secondly, embedded machine vision technology refers to the integration of vision systems directly into machines or devices. Lastly, smart cameras-based machine vision technology involves the use of cameras with built-in processing power and intelligence to perform image analysis tasks independently. Each type of technology offers its own advantages and is suited for various applications in industries like manufacturing, automotive, and healthcare.</p></p>
