<p><p>Steam shrink tunnels are used in packaging industry to shrink wrap products with the help of steam. There are two main types of steam shrink tunnels in the market - Single Zone Tunnels and Dual Zone Tunnels. Single zone tunnels have one section where steam is applied evenly to shrink the film around the product. Dual zone tunnels have two sections - one for applying steam and the other for cooling the product quickly after shrinking. Both types provide efficient and effective packaging solutions for various industries.</p></p>
