Round head bolts have a smooth and rounded head, making them suitable for applications where a more finished appearance is desired. Square head bolts have a four-sided head that provides a larger contact area, making them easier to grip and tighten. Each type of bolt serves a specific purpose based on the requirements of the earth moving equipment.</p></p>
