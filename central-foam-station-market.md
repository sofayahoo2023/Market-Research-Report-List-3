<p><p>Central foam stations come in three main types based on the number of nozzles they have: with one nozzle, with two nozzles, and with three nozzles. Stations with one nozzle are suitable for smaller areas or specific spot applications. Stations with two nozzles are ideal for medium-sized locations that require more coverage. Stations with three nozzles provide even more foam dispersal and are typically used in larger industrial settings or where high volumes of foam are needed for fire suppression.</p></p>
