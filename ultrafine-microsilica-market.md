<p><p>Ultrafine MicroSilica, also known as silica fume, is categorized into three different types based on density: Densified Silica Fume, Semi Densified Silica Fume, and Undensified Silica Fume. Densified Silica Fume has the highest density and is typically used in high-performance concrete applications. Semi Densified Silica Fume falls in the middle in terms of density and is commonly used in a variety of construction materials. Undensified Silica Fume has the lowest density and is often incorporated into applications where flowability is a key factor.</p></p>
