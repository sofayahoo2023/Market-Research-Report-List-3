<p><p>Organic fertilizers can be categorized into three main types: plant-based, animal-based, and mineral-based. Plant-based organic fertilizers are derived from composted plant material, such as seaweed, grass clippings, or vegetable scraps. Animal-based organic fertilizers are made from animal by-products, like manure or bone meal. Mineral-based organic fertilizers consist of naturally occurring rock minerals that provide essential nutrients for plants. Each type of organic fertilizer offers a unique set of benefits and can help improve soil health and promote plant growth in a sustainable manner.</p></p>
