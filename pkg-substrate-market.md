<p><p>PKG Substrate market can be broadly categorized into two types - Total Ceramics PKG and Non-Total Ceramics PKG. Total Ceramics PKG includes substrates made entirely of ceramics, offering high thermal conductivity and reliability for demanding applications. On the other hand, Non-Total Ceramics PKG involves substrates made of a combination of materials such as ceramics, metals, and polymers, providing a cost-effective solution while still maintaining good thermal performance. Both types cater to different market segments based on their unique characteristics and applications.</p></p>
