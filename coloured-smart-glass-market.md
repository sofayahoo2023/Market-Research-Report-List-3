<p><strong>The Coloured Smart Glass Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electrochromic Smart Glass</li><li>SPD Smart Glass</li><li>PDLC Smart Glass</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Coloured Smart Glass is available in three major types: Electrochromic Smart Glass, SPD Smart Glass, and PDLC Smart Glass. Electrochromic Smart Glass tints in response to an electrical charge, providing privacy and sun control. SPD Smart Glass uses suspended particle technology to adjust tint levels, offering customizable shading and glare reduction. PDLC Smart Glass uses a liquid crystal film to switch between transparent and opaque states, creating privacy on demand. These different types of Smart Glass cater to various needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
