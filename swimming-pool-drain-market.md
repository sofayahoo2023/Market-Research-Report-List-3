<p><strong>The Swimming Pool Drain Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gravity Drainage</li><li>Multiple Main Drains</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>There are several types of swimming pool drain markets, with the main ones being gravity drainage, multiple main drains, and others. Gravity drainage is the most common type, where water naturally flows down through the drain due to gravity. Multiple main drains are designed to prevent suction entrapment by dispersing water flow across several drains. The "Others" category includes alternative drain designs such as channel drains or slot drains. Each type of drain market serves a specific purpose in ensuring a safe and efficient water circulation system in swimming pools.</p></p>
