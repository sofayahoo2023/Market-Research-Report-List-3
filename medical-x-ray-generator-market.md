<p><p>The medical X-ray generator market consists of high frequency X-ray generators and low frequency X-ray generators. High frequency X-ray generators utilize a high frequency electrical current to produce X-rays with greater efficiency and precision. They are commonly used in advanced medical imaging and have a smaller size and higher operating frequency. On the other hand, low frequency X-ray generators produce X-rays using a lower frequency electrical current. They are typically used in simpler medical imaging applications and have a larger size and lower operating frequency.</p></p>
