<p><p>Pallet Flow Racking is a type of storage system that uses inclined rollers or wheels to transport pallets within a warehouse. The different market types of Pallet Flow Racking include Steel Skatewheel, Poly Wheel, Magnum Wheel, Full-Width Roller, and Others. Steel Skatewheel utilizes steel wheels for better durability and strength. Poly Wheel incorporates polyurethane wheels for smoother movement and noise reduction. Magnum Wheel features larger wheels for heavy-load applications. Full-Width Roller uses rollers that cover the full width of pallets, providing excellent support and stability. Other types may include variations or combinations of these features, offering unique benefits based on specific warehouse requirements.</p></p>
