<p><p>Polymer electrolyte membranes are categorized into three types based on their structure: Single Layer, Multi Layer, and Others. Single Layer membranes are made up of a single layer of polymer, providing simplicity and cost-effectiveness. Multi Layer membranes consist of multiple layers of polymers, offering improved performance and durability. Other types of membranes utilize different materials or structures, such as ceramic fillers or composite materials. Each type of membrane has its own advantages and is used in various applications within the fuel cell industry.</p></p>
