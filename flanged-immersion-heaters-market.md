<p><p>Flanged immersion heaters are types of heaters that are designed to be inserted into tanks or containers to heat up liquids. There are two main types of flanged immersion heaters: straight flanged immersion heaters and L-shaped flanged immersion heaters. </p><p>Straight flanged immersion heaters have a straight tube-shaped design, making them easy to install and suitable for heating larger volumes of liquid. </p><p>On the other hand, L-shaped flanged immersion heaters have a bent shape, which allows them to be used in applications with limited space or when there are obstructions in the tank. They are typically used for heating smaller volumes of liquid.</p></p>
