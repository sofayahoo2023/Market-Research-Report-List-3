<p><ul><li>1×2</li><li>1×4</li><li>1×8</li><li>2×2</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multimode fiber couplers are optical devices used to split or combine optical signals in multiple directions. The market types 1×2, 1×4, 1×8, and 2×2 refer to the configurations of these couplers. A 1×2 coupler splits a single input into two outputs, while 1×4 and 1×8 couplers split the input into four and eight outputs respectively. On the other hand, a 2×2 coupler combines two inputs into two outputs. These different configurations cater to various networking and communication requirements in the industry.</p></p>
