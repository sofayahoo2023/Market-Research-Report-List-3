<p><p>Conductive Polymer Hybrid Capacitors come in two main types: SMD Type and Radial Lead Type. SMD Type capacitors are surface-mounted devices that are compact and ideal for high-density circuit boards. On the other hand, Radial Lead Type capacitors have leads that come out from the sides and are suitable for through-hole mounting on PCBs. Both types offer efficient energy storage and superior conductivity, making them popular choices in various electronic applications.</p></p>
